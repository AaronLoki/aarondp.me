# General Resources

A curated collection of helpful resources for IB students.

- [IB Official Website](https://www.ibo.org/)

- IB Past Paper Store

- Citation generators (MLA, APA, Chicago)

## Exam Preparation

### Timeline Planning

- Create a study schedule early
- Allocate time for each subject based on difficulty
- Include regular review sessions
- Plan breaks and rest days

### Effective Study Techniques

- **Active Recall**: Test yourself regularly
- **Spaced Repetition**: Review content over time
- **Practice Papers**: Simulate exam conditions
- **Group Study**: Collaborate with peers

## Time Management

- Use planners or digital calendars
- Break large tasks into smaller steps
- Prioritize based on deadlines and difficulty
- Balance IA, EE, CAS, and subject work

## Wellbeing

Remember to:
- Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours)
- Maintain a healthy diet
- Exercise regularly
- Take breaks during study sessions
- Ask for help when needed
